Our Story

Golden Ocean for Investment and Trade was founded with the intention of creating a trusted, sustainable, and high-quality service platform across multiple sectors. Over the years, we have built strong capabilities in fish trading, logistics services, and vehicle rentals, ensuring seamless support for our clients across markets.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized leader in investment and trade, offering reliable, innovative, and customer-centric solutions across all our sectors.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality resources, dependable logistics, and exceptional customer service while building long-term relationships based on reliability and trust.

OUR SERVICES

FISH TRADING

We specialize in sourcing and supplying high-quality fresh and frozen fish from trusted fisheries. Our team ensures strict quality control, proper storage, and fast delivery to guarantee freshness. Whether you are a restaurant, retailer, or wholesale buyer, we ensure timely supply and competitive pricing.

Logistics & Transportation

We offer reliable logistics solutions that make transportation smooth and efficient. Our logistics team ensures safe handling, on-time deliveries, and seamless movement of goods within local and regional networks. From small parcels to bulk shipments, we manage everything end-to-end.

Car Rental Services

Our car rental division provides well-maintained, fully serviced vehicles for personal, corporate, and travel use. Customers can choose from a wide range of cars—economy, luxury, and SUVs—offered with flexible rental plans. Safety, cleanliness, and comfort are our top priorities.
